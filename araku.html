Araku

Araku Valley is a picturesque hill station located in the Eastern Ghats, near Visakhapatnam, famous for its scenic landscapes, coffee plantations, and tranquil environment. The valley is known for its cool climate, making it a perfect getaway from the city's hustle and bustle.

Visitors to Araku can explore the coffee plantations, enjoy trekking trails, and experience the local tribal culture. The valley also offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills, waterfalls, and lush greenery, making it a paradise for nature lovers.

Araku Valley is a popular destination for tourists seeking a peaceful retreat, and its unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage makes it an unforgettable experience.
