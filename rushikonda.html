Rushikonda

Rushikonda Beach, located in Visakhapatnam, is one of the most stunning beaches in the region, known for its golden sands, clear waters, and peaceful environment. It's a popular spot for both relaxation and water sports.

The beach offers activities like parasailing, jet skiing, and windsurfing, attracting adventure enthusiasts. The surrounding hills and lush greenery provide an ideal backdrop for photography and nature walks.

With its laid-back ambiance and beautiful sunsets, Rushikonda is a must-visit destination for beach lovers, families, and anyone looking to escape into nature's tranquility.
