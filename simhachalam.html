Simhachalam

Simhachalam is a prominent hilltop temple located near Visakhapatnam, dedicated to Lord Narasimha, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The temple is known for its stunning architecture and the intricate carvings that adorn its structure.

The temple sits atop a hill and provides panoramic views of the surrounding city and landscape. It is an important pilgrimage site, attracting devotees throughout the year, especially during the annual Brahmotsavam festival.

Simhachalam is also famous for its religious significance, with many devotees visiting to seek blessings from Lord Narasimha for health, prosperity, and protection.
