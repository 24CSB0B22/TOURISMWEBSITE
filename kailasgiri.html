Kailasgiri

Kailasgiri is one of the most iconic landmarks in Visakhapatnam, known for its serene hilltop location and breathtaking views of the city and the Bay of Bengal. It is a popular tourist destination that offers a perfect blend of nature, spirituality, and leisure.

The hilltop is adorned with a massive statue of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, making it a spiritual spot for devotees. Visitors can also enjoy activities like the ropeway ride, which provides a panoramic view of the lush greenery and surrounding landscapes, and the toy train that offers a fun ride around the hill.

With its landscaped gardens, refreshing ambiance, and ample spots for relaxation, Kailasgiri serves as an ideal getaway for families, couples, and nature enthusiasts alike.
