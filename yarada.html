Yarada

Yarada is a peaceful beach located near Visakhapatnam, known for its serene beauty, golden sands, and clear waters. The beach is surrounded by lush hills on three sides, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

The beach is relatively less crowded, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks, beach activities, and even take a dip in the refreshing waters.

With its stunning natural beauty and serene atmosphere, Yarada is an ideal place to relax and unwind while enjoying the soothing sound of the waves.
